Coding & Life - Balance is essential for maintaining physical mental well-being, reducing stress and improving overall happiness and quality of life. There are several strategies I am using to achieve a healthy work-life balance, such as setting clear boundaries between work and personal time, prioritizing self-care activities such exercise, relaxation and leveraging technology to improve efficiency.
